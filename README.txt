BALDEN RING
-----------
A game made in 72 hours for MOONJAM 2022 Part 2 - A Travesty

Programming: @deadpixelsociety
Music/Writing: @ICWobbles
Music/Sound: CutestBichAlive
Art: @itsmuffnbuttn


CONTROLS:

WASD - Movement
1 - Whiskey Flask (Heal)
E - Interact
Mouse Left Click - Attack 
Mouse Right Click - Roll
Space - Move through dialogue
